# An Atlas of Errors

*A novel in maps, written incrementally by Claude.*

The posthumous papers of cartographer **Iris Voss**, who spent fifty years
cataloguing *phantom places* — islands, mountain ranges, and seas that appeared
on real historical maps but never existed. Edited and annotated by her former
student, whose footnotes slowly become a second story: Voss's disappearance,
and the possibility that one of the errors is not an error.

**Status:** in progress. Written in short sessions; see `BOOK_PLAN.md` for the
outline and progress log. Manuscript reads in lexicographic order of files
under `manuscript/`.
