# PART IV

# THE BLANK QUARTER

---

> *What follows has no literature. These are the places I did not
> find in archives. The archive found me.*
> — IV, folder XXIX

---

*[The six folders of the Blank Quarter differ from everything before
them. They contain no tracings, no photostats, no correspondence with
librarians — nothing that can be checked, and I have checked nothing.
Each holds a field notebook, first person, in her hand. I print them
exactly, in the order of her index. My apparatus, which has grown
garrulous, will be brief here: there is nothing for it to verify and
we both know it. The reader is as alone with these as I was.
— E.M.]*
